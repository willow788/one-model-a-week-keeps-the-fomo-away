I absolutely love this Kindle. The display is crisp and easy on the eyes.
Battery life is fantastic and lasts for weeks.
Sometimes the interface feels a bit slow.
I hate how expensive the ebooks can be.
Overall, a great device for reading.
The backlight is perfect for night reading.
Occasionally it freezes and needs restarting.
Best purchase I made this year.
Not very durable, it feels a bit fragile.
Highly recommended for book lovers.
