I Love You
When I think of you, my heart skips a beat. I journey through my mind, gasp at the thought of what a journey life with you would be, always an adventure, always inquisitive, and always up for a laugh.

I can’t lose sight of the fact that you remind me of myself in many ways, very flirty, very reflective, very funny, very sensual, and up for adventure, always; these are the similar adjectives I’d use to describe us both.

Life is not out to get us; in fact, I’d say we are the product of our choices, intentions, and interactions that brought us together. As we continue to evolve as individuals and come together, may we continue to make choices that bring us closer.

In reality, that’s the goal: to bring us closer. In timelines and through energy and entanglement, I don’t want to push you past your limits, yet I want to keep moving so we can keep working to understand each other, reflecting more and more of each other.

My truth is waking up to a reality where we conquer the matrix together.
